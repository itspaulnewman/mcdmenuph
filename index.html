Overview of Popular Breakfast Items

Local FavoritesMcDonald's Philippines offers a range of breakfast items that cater specifically to local tastes. Here are some of the most popular options:

  • Cheesy Eggdesal with Sausage Solo: This Filipino-inspired breakfast sandwich features a sausage patty, egg, cheese, and mayonnaise on a toasted pandesal bun, priced at ₱108
  • Longganisa with Egg Meal: Combines the traditional Filipino sausage, longganisa, with egg and rice, available for ₱96 solo and ₱154 small meal
  • Chicken McDo with Rice: A classic Filipino breakfast option featuring crispy fried chicken served with steamed rice.

Global Classics

While local flavors are a hit, international favorites also have a strong following:

  • Sausage McMuffin with Egg: A classic McDonald's breakfast sandwich made with a sausage patty, egg, and cheese on a toasted English muffin, priced at ₱144 with hash browns

  • Hotcakes: Two fluffy buttermilk pancakes served with butter and syrup, a popular choice among both kids and adults, priced at ₱86 for a solo order
